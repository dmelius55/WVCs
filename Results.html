How to Read These Hotspot Cards

Each card summarizes environmental and structural conditions at our 13 wildlife–vehicle collision hotspot locations.

Colored dots show how well each site supports wildlife movement and stream function.

🟢 Green (3): Conditions are good and closely match natural stream and wildlife needs.
🟡 Yellow (2): Conditions are functional but partially limiting.
🔴 Red (1): Conditions strongly limit wildlife movement or natural stream processes.
💧 Aquatic Organism Passage (AOP): Ability for aquatic species to move through the structure.
🛠️ Structural Condition (SC): Physical condition of the culvert or crossing.
🌍 Geomorphic Compatibility (GC): How well the structure matches natural stream shape and flow.
