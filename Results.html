How to Read These Hotspot Cards

Each card summarizes conditions at a wildlife–vehicle collision hotspot. Scores combine ecological impact, infrastructure condition, and movement barriers to identify locations where mitigation would provide the greatest benefit.

🐾 Aquatic Organism Passage: Ability for fish and other aquatic species to move through the structure.
🏗 Structural Condition: Physical condition of the culvert or crossing.
🌍 Geomorphic Compatibility: How well the structure matches natural stream shape and flow.
🟢 Open Ratio: Width of structure relative to stream width (lower = more restrictive).
